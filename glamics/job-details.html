Agricultural Drone Pilot

KITEMAPAS AERIAL MAPPING SERVICES PVT LTD.
Location
Hyderabad
   
Job type
Full Time

Job Description

We are seeking a skilled and detail-oriented Drone Pilot to support our agricultural drone operations in Karnataka. The role will focus on executing precise flight operations for agricultural drone spraying, including fertilization, pesticide application, and crop monitoring. The ideal candidate will have a strong understanding of drone operations, a willingness to travel to rural and semi-urban areas, and proficiency in the local language to communicate effectively with farmers and stakeholders.

Responsibilities:

  • Conduct safe and efficient drone flight operations for agricultural spraying (pesticides, fertilizers) and crop monitoring.
  • Operate and maintain agricultural drones, ensuring optimal performance and compliance with safety guidelines.
  • Plan and execute drone missions based on field requirements and predefined flight parameters.
  • Perform pre-flight, in-flight, and post-flight inspections to ensure operational safety and equipment readiness.
  • Coordinate with farmers and field teams to assess spraying needs and communicate operational plans effectively.
